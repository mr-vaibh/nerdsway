NERDSWAY

 NerdsWay is an Indian non-profit organization that publishes classified media provided by the people of the community. It provides media, publication, news, reportage and satirical commentary. It was founded in 2021 by Vaibhav Shukla in India under it's parent company MRVAIBH Inc., started in 2019 that had provided multi-sector benefits in its first 2 years.

Transparent and open source, NerdsWay is a platform for the public to share their knowledge, views and opinions. It publishes pertinent articles which can also be voted by regular users of the internet. It is different from other publications as it's 100% non-profit and does not earn money, the only vision behind NW is to provide a free platform for free voice.

What NT is NOT?

NerdsWay is not a propaganda or propaganda-related website. It is not meant to spread extreme hate (or even love) on government, politics or any cultural belief.

non-profit.

The organization is independent of any source of revenue other than BuyMeACoffee.com support. There are NO ads, as we don't intend to sell our supporters' data and our self-esteem to advertisers. We do not demand any other donation than your precious time and support.

free voice.

Not for the sake of saying, but for the sake of democracy, we actually believe in free voice. All the blogs, media and other terms of information go through our Editing Policies. Come, join us, we HAVE to save our Right to Freedom of Speech culture.

neutrality meter.

Like no other media company, we do have a fool-proof methodology to calculate the neutrality (how unbiased) of an information. The scale is between 1 to 10 and literally anyone (who is an NTian) can vote for it. Also, there are no chances of spam by creating multiple accounts, our system is capable of handling those situations.

privacy.

In a very simple and laymans' language we have justified our Policies. Your data/information is safe and our sources are anonymous. We DO NOT take privacy lightly.
